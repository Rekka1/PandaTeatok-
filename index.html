Home Workout Schedule

Senin - Arms and Chest

Program Latihan:

  • Push-Ups: 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Tricep Dips on Chair: 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Wide Grip Push-Ups: 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Alternating Dumbbell Bicep Curls ( Use a bag if you don't have dumbbells ): 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Plank: 3 sets of 30 seconds

Selasa - Back Shoulders and Abs

Program Latihan:

  • Bodyweight Rows (using a sturdy table): 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Superman Exercise: 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Bicycle Crunches: 3 sets of 20 reps
  • Reverse Crunches: 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Plank: 3 sets of 30 seconds

Rabu - Leg

Program Latihan:

  • Bodyweight Squats: 4 sets of 20 reps
  • Lunges: 3 sets of 15 reps per leg
  • Wall Sit: 3 sets of 1 minute
  • Calf Raises: 4 sets of 20 reps

Kamis - Arms and Chest

Program Latihan:

  • Push-Ups: 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Tricep Dips on Chair: 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Diamond Push-Ups: 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Bodyweight Tricep Extensions: 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Plank: 3 sets of 30 seconds

Jumat - Back Shoulders and Abs

Program Latihan:

  • Bodyweight Rows (using a sturdy table): 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Superman Exercise: 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Bicycle Crunches: 3 sets of 20 reps
  • Reverse Crunches: 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Plank: 3 sets of 30 seconds

Sabtu - Leg

Program Latihan:

  • Bodyweight Squats: 4 sets of 20 reps
  • Lunges: 3 sets of 15 reps per leg
  • Wall Sit: 3 sets of 1 minute
  • Calf Raises: 4 sets of 20 reps

Minggu - Cardio atau Rest

Pilih sesi cardio atau istirahat untuk hari Minggu.

Nutrisi Harian

Asupan Nutrisi:

  • Protein: 120g per day
  • Kalori: 2000 per day
  • Serat: 25g per day
  • Air: 8 glasses per day

MOTIVATION

Motivasi 1:

Bangun, latih tubuhmu, dan raih impianmu. Kesuksesan dimulai dari langkah pertama.

Motivasi 2:

Ketika latihan terasa berat, ingatlah: setiap usaha yang kau lakukan hari ini adalah investasi untuk kesehatanmu esok hari.

Motivasi 3:

Tak ada batasan bagi kemampuanmu. Percayalah pada dirimu sendiri dan teruslah bergerak maju.

Motivasi 4:

Latihanmu hari ini adalah versi terbaik dari dirimu kemarin. Pertahankan semangatmu!

Motivasi 5:

Ketika rasa malas datang, ingatlah mengapa kau memulai. Transformasi dimulai dari keteguhan hatimu.
